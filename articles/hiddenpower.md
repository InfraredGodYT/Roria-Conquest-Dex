## Atlus
IVs are listed in the standard arrangement of HP/Atk/Def/SpA/SpD/Spe.
O corresponds to Odd and E corresponds to even.
Two listings are provided, the first as whether or not IVs must be odd or even and the second as the highest possible IVs for each Hidden Power.
Note for new players: There is no Hidden Power Normal or Hidden Power Fairy
