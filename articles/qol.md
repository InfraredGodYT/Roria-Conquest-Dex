# QOL Changes

These article details all of the Quality of Life (QOL) changes added to Roria Conquest.

### Adventure Changes

<ul style="line-height: 1.7;">
  <li>Trainers give 2x Money &amp; can be rematched without rejoining</li>
  <li>Arcade gives 5x more Tix</li>
  <li>Permanent 2x Shiny Odds (1/2048 base odds, 1/1024 with Shiny Charm)</li>
  <li>Permanent 2x HA Odds (1/256)</li>
  <li>Happiness/Friendship grinding is 5x faster</li>
  <li>Oval Charm buffed from 15% to 25% Egg Cycles reduced</li>
  <li>Breeding passes down HAs from Mother/Non-Ditto Parent (60% chance with Ability Charm, 30% without Ability Charm)</li>
  <li>Alternate Gamemodes (Nuzlocke, Randomizer, etc) can bypass HMs</li>
  <li>Multiple Rare Candies, Potions, Vitamins, &amp; EV Feathers can be used at once</li>
  <li>Eggs are sent to the PC if your party is full</li>
  <li>Item limit increased to 999</li>
  <li>2nd floor of Pokémon Center contains the Move Reminder, Stone Shop, Move Tutor, &amp; Hyper Trainer</li>
</ul>

### Shop Changes

<ul style="line-height: 1.7;">
  <li>All Mega Stones available in the Stone Shop</li>
  <li>All Evolution Items available in the Stone Shop</li>
  <li>Rare Candy available in the Regular Shop (4 Badges required)</li>
  <li>EV Resets available in the Regular Shop (4 Badges required)</li>
  <li>BP Shop prices reworked (e.g., TMs now cost 30–70 BP)</li>
  <li>Berry Shop added for Alternate Gamemodes in Rosecove City</li>
</ul>


### Colosseum Changes

<ul style="line-height: 1.7;">
  <li>BP range increased to 11–15 (formerly 1–5)</li>
  <li>BP limit increased to 9,999,999</li>
  <li>Receive 3 Bottle Caps and 2 Ability Patches for every 5 wins (12-hour cooldown)</li>
  <li>2× BP for every 5 wins when streak rewards are on cooldown</li>
  <li>Move Relearner costs $5,000 (formerly $30,000), free in Alternate Gamemodes</li>
  <li>Additional PvP formats added (OU, UUbers, Ubers, etc.)</li>
</ul>
