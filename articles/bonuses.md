# Daily Bonuses

Roria Conquest provides daily benefits for its players. These all stack with their respective Ro-Powers. Any decimals are rounded down.

### Monday
1.5x Experience Points
### Tuesday
1.5x Money (Pokedollars)
### Wednesday
1.5x Egg Steps
### Thursday
1.5x EVs
### Friday, Saturday, Sunday
1.5x Shiny Odds
